Information & Reminders

Important instructions for qualifiers reporting to SLSU Satellite Campuses.

📌 Important Reminders

  1. Only applicants included in the satellite campus qualifier list and scheduled for the date shown in the checker will be entertained.
  2. Report to the satellite campus indicated in your result. Do not proceed to another campus unless officially advised.
  3. Bring your Examination Permit, admission forms, and required supporting documents for verification.
  4. College applicants are required to wear a plain white shirt and maong pants when reporting to the campus.
  5. No reservation fees are collected to confirm an admission slot.
  6. Non-appearance on the given schedule may result in forfeiture of the slot.

📅 Reporting Schedule

Schedule Notice: Satellite campus schedules are individualized. Use the checker to view your assigned campus and date.

  • Venue: The SLSU Satellite Campus shown in your result.
  • Please arrive early and follow the instructions of the campus admission personnel.

📋 General Admission Requirements

Primary Documents

  • Examination Permit duly signed by the test administrator
  • Filled out admission forms
  • Original and photocopy of Grade 12 Report Card or applicable academic record

Supporting Documents

  • 2x2 picture with nametag and white background
  • Original and photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Original and photocopy of Good Moral Character
  • Other documents required by the assigned satellite campus

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What will the checker show?

It will show your assigned satellite campus, your first-choice course, and the date when you should report to that campus.

What if my application number is not found?

It means the number is not included in the current satellite campus qualifier list loaded in this checker. Please wait for official announcements or contact the admission office.

Can I report to a different satellite campus?

No. Applicants should report to the satellite campus indicated in their result unless officially instructed otherwise.

Should I screenshot my result?

Yes. Keep a screenshot of your result as proof of your assigned campus and schedule.
